Is There Still Hope?

The earth's sky, contrasted with the black sky of the cosmos, harmonizes with bright and cheerful colors, presenting me with a feeling of absolute freedom as if soaring through the air. I paint the soft blue sky and the various forms of clouds, instilling them with the expectation and possibility that our dreams and aspirations can be realized at any time.

The clouds that form the center of the painting are not fixed entities. They contain the flexible journey of life that changes at any moment. These clouds symbolize the many transformations we experience in life, reminding us that hope always exists even amidst hardship and adversity. The moment we look up at the sky, infinite possibilities and the opportunity to start anew are hidden within it.

In the journey of life, we can ceaselessly seek out hope, and that path is always open to us.
