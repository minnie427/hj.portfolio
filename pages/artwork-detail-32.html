When Joy Creates the Curve

This painting is an expression of the flow when joy arises, depicted through lines. Within the unforced curves and naturally flowing lines, a pure energy is contained. These lines fill the space, dancing as if following a rhythm.

Joy is not expressed through straight lines. It is a curve, a circle, and the revolving energy of life force. I captured that energy on the brush tip and painted where my heart led me. Without any fixed format or preconceived composition, relying solely on line and color.

Only upon completing the painting did I finally understand: all of this was the curve generated by the joy within me. My heart moved, that movement became a line, and those lines converged to form the most genuine single emotion.
