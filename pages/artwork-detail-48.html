Awakening in Stillness

This painting captures the moment when the true self awakens within a stillness that transcends all external noise. Against the dark night sky, I sit in the posture of a practitioner, embracing the intense light and energy that rises from my core. The dynamic curves and vibrant colors represent the inner movement and a dance toward liberation, where tranquility and intensity merge. Enlightenment does not come from the outside. Only by halting all senses and listening quietly does it finally fill one completely from within. This work declares that stillness is the very source of the most powerful energy.
