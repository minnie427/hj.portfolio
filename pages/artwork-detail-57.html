Golden Start

This painting commemorates the golden start that arrived in my life, at the moment when everything begins to bloom anew. The warm golden light filling the canvas symbolizes infinite possibilities, while below, blue flowers quietly form waves of hope. Gazing at that light, I feel a trembling anticipation for the future and the hidden potential within me. This painting is my resolve, expressing the courage to dispel darkness and radiate one's own light. I earnestly hope that this golden energy permeates the viewer's heart, conveying the warm courage to begin anew.
