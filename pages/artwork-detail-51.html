Three Eyes, Guardian of the Soul

This painting is my record of invoking the guardian of the soul, a mysterious bird with three eyes, onto the canvas. Resembling a heavenly spirit passed down from antiquity, this being becomes a warm shield that comforts me and keeps negative energy far away. The three eyes illuminate with the light of wisdom across the ages, firmly holding my wavering heart steady. I feel the sublime and powerful aura of protection surrounding me from this mystical being. This work proclaims that a wide-awake guardian always exists in our deepest core, and that presence grants me peace and courage.
